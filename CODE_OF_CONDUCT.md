# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving and accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the project

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to **support@askalf.org**. All reports will be reviewed and will result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances.
