# AI & Tech Conferences - Q1 2026

## Priority Conferences for Ask ALF

### 1. CES 2026
- **Dates:** January 7-10, 2026
- **Location:** Las Vegas, NV
- **Cost:** ~$300-500 (varies by pass type)
- **Why Attend:** World's largest consumer tech show. Great for visibility, media coverage, and meeting potential enterprise customers.
- **Booth Cost:** $30,000+ for Eureka Park (startup area)
- **Best For:** Brand awareness, press coverage
- **Register:** ces.tech

### 2. AI Conference - Menlo Park
- **Dates:** February 4-5, 2026
- **Location:** Menlo Park, CA (Silicon Valley)
- **Cost:** TBD
- **Why Attend:** Heart of VC territory. Great for investor connections.
- **Best For:** Fundraising, networking with investors

### 3. DeveloperWeek 2026
- **Dates:** February 11-13, 2026 (in-person), February 18-20 (online)
- **Location:** San Francisco, CA
- **Cost:** ~$500-800
- **Why Attend:** Developer-focused audience. Perfect for API product demos.
- **Best For:** Developer adoption, technical partnerships
- **Register:** developerweek.com

### 4. Gartner CIO Leadership Forum
- **Dates:** February 23-24, 2026
- **Location:** Phoenix, AZ
- **Cost:** ~$3,500+
- **Why Attend:** Enterprise decision-makers. Good for B2B sales leads.
- **Best For:** Enterprise sales, CIO relationships

### 5. NVIDIA GTC 2026 ⭐ (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
- **Dates:** March 16-19, 2026
- **Location:** San Jose, CA
- **Cost:** $2,172-$2,525
- **Why Attend:** "Woodstock of AI" - premier event for AI developers and researchers. Hundreds of sessions on generative AI, robotics, industrial AI.
- **Best For:** Technical credibility, AI community presence
- **Special:** NVIDIA Inception program for startups (free) - gives access to developer resources, preferred pricing, VC exposure
- **Register:** nvidia.com/gtc

## Speaking Opportunity Tips

1. **Submit CFPs early** - Most close 3-6 months before
2. **Focus on technical content** - "How we built X" > "What we do"
3. **Include environmental angle** - Differentiates from other AI talks
4. **Offer workshops** - Higher acceptance rate than keynotes
